# Themes

## Themes

**Spada** has 2 themes:

- Spada

- Dark Spada

### Choosing the Theme

Menu Options \> Config

![](../reference/figures/articles/themes/themes_menu.png)

Open **Theme**

![](../reference/figures/articles/themes/themes_config.png)

Choose the desired Theme and **Apply**. The app will be updated to the
choosed theme.

![](../reference/figures/articles/themes/themes_dark_spada.png)
